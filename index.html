Biography

Currently, I am a research assistant of Machine Intellignece Lab (MiLAB) in Westlake University, advised by Prof. Donglin Wang. I received my B.S. degree in the Department of Intelligence Science and Technology (IST) in June 2022 from Northeast Electric Power University (NEEPU), advised by Prof. Xinhua Wang, Prof. Hongyin Ni, Prof. Bing Li, and Prof. Qingzhu Wang.

Research Interests

My current main research works focus on building machine learning models that are robust and adaptable to distribution shifts. The following topics are studied to revolve around this goal:

  • Object Detection

  • Edge Computing

  • Path Planning
